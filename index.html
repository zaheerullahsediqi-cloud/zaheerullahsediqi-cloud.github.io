About Kohinoor

Built to be the supplier contracting officers can count on twice.

Kohinoor Suppliers Inc is a San Antonio, Texas-based supply and contracting company. Rather than specializing in a single product line, we're built to source, quote, and deliver against a wide range of solicitations — matching what buyers actually need instead of what fits a narrow catalog.
